String Kernel Classification

String kernel based text classification: identifying political affiliation

The aim of this project is to understand the underlying mathematical principles of the kernel trick by applying it to a simple concrete problem. Microblogging sites such as Twitter have become important forums for discussion during this election year. Political figures, such as members of congress and state legislatures, and officials both at the Federal and state level tweet regularly to communicate with the public. Our goal will be to use kernel methods to predict whether a tweet comes from a republican or a democrat, by doing a binary classification of the tweets.
